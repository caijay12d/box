Solid Bleached Sulfate (SBS) Paperboard

Our solid bleached sulfate (SBS) paperboard offers superior print sharpness, rigidity, and food-contact safety. Ideal for pharmaceutical and cosmetic packaging.

Sustainable Materials & Green Manufacturing

Eco-friendly packaging that meets global environmental standards.

FSC-Certified Paperboard

All our SBS and kraft paperboard can be sourced with FSC certification, ensuring responsible forest management and full traceability.

Recycled Fibers

Post-consumer recycled (PCR) content options available up to 100%, reducing environmental footprint without compromising structural integrity.

Plastic-Free Coatings
