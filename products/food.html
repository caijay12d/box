Frequently Asked Questions

Are your food boxes FDA and EU food-contact compliant?

Yes. All our SBS paperboard used for food packaging meets FDA 21 CFR and EU Regulation 1935/2004 requirements. We can provide compliance documentation upon request.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our standard MOQ is 5,000 pieces per size/design. For smaller test runs, we can accommodate 2,000 pieces with a surcharge. Contact us for details.

Can you add a grease-resistant or moisture barrier coating?

Absolutely. We offer grease-resistant coatings for bakery and snack boxes, as well as moisture barrier linings for products that require extra protection. These are food-safe and compliant.
