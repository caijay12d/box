Frequently Asked Questions

Can you match my brand's Pantone color?

Yes. We offer Pantone (PMS) color matching as part of our printing process. Simply provide your Pantone code and we will ensure accurate color reproduction on your boxes.

What special finishes are available for beauty boxes?

We offer soft-touch lamination, spot UV, embossing/debossing, gold/silver foil stamping, holographic foil, and PVC/PET window patches. Combinations are available.

Do you provide structural design for custom shapes?

Yes. Our in-house team can create custom dielines and structural designs. We provide 3D renderings for approval before sampling. This service is free for orders above 10,000 pcs.
