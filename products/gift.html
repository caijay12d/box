Frequently Asked Questions

Can you produce custom inserts for gift boxes?

Yes. We offer EVA foam, sponge, and cardboard inserts custom-cut to fit your product. Inserts can be wrapped in velvet, satin, or paper to match the box interior.

What is the lead time for rigid gift boxes?

Sampling takes 7–10 days for rigid boxes (longer than folding cartons due to hand-assembly). Mass production is 20–30 days after approval.

Do you offer holiday or seasonal gift box designs?

Yes. We can produce seasonal designs with custom printing, ribbons, and decorative elements. We recommend ordering 60 days before your seasonal launch date.
