Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer child-resistant (CR) packaging?

Yes. We manufacture CR packaging that meets 16 CFR §1700.20 (US) and EN 14375 (EU) standards. We can provide CR test reports from certified labs.

What is the minimum order quantity for pharma boxes?

Standard MOQ is 5,000 pcs. For regulated packaging requiring special tooling (e.g., CR closures), the MOQ may be 10,000 pcs. Contact us for a specific quote.

What is the typical lead time for pharma packaging?

Sample production takes 5–7 business days. Mass production is 15–25 days after sample approval. Rush orders can be accommodated with prior arrangement.
