Quality Control Process

Every order goes through our 3-layer quality inspection system before shipment:

  • Layer 1 – Raw material check: Paperboard weight, brightness, and food-contact compliance verified before production.
  • Layer 2 – In-line inspection: Print color accuracy, die-cutting precision, and folding alignment monitored during production.
  • Layer 3 – Pre-shipment audit: Full carton check, glue strength, and visual defect scan. Photo report sent to client for approval.
